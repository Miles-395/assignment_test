Ford Motor Company, founded in 1903 by Henry Ford, is one of the world’s most iconic automotive brands, known for making cars accessible, reliable, and innovative. Among its most legendary creations is the Ford Mustang, introduced in 1964 and instantly recognized as a symbol of American performance and freedom. The Mustang blends bold design with powerful engineering, evolving over the decades while staying true to its muscle car heritage. Today, it represents Ford’s commitment to performance, technology, and timeless automotive culture.
